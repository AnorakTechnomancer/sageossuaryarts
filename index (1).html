The Artist

✦

Welcome to Sage Ossuary Arts — a studio born from a love of the strange, the beautiful, and the forgotten. Working across canvas, digital illustration, and found bone, each piece is an act of reverence for the liminal spaces between the living world and what lies beyond.

Art here is made with intention — steeped in folk magic, natural history, and the quiet power of things that endure. Whether it's pigment on linen or a careful arrangement of bone, every work carries something of the wild and the otherworldly.
